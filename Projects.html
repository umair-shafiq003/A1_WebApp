1. Programming 2: Object Oriented Programming:

Project: Online Food Ordering System; HUNGER DELIGHTS .CO

The Online Food Ordering and Reservation System is a Python-based solution facilitating convenient food ordering and table reservations at restaurants. Users can create accounts, browse menus, place orders for delivery or pickup, and make table reservations seamlessly. Restaurant owners have access to management tools for menus, orders, and reservations, while administrators oversee the system's operation. With features like real-time order tracking and analytics, the system enhances the dining experience for customers and optimizes operations for restaurants.

2. Database: Using SQL:

Project Title: Automotive Service Management System

The Automotive Service Management System streamlines vehicle maintenance operations, including service scheduling, parts procurement, and payment tracking. It manages invoices, payments, employee details, service records, warranties, and insurance information. Key features include: Payment and Invoice Management: Tracks payments and generates invoices. Employee and Service Management: Records employee details and service history. Insurance and Warranty Tracking: Manages insurance policies and warranty information. Vehicle and Customer Management: Stores vehicle and customer details. Part and Purchase Order Management: Tracks parts inventory and purchase orders. Supplier Management: Manages supplier details and procurement processes.

3. Machine Learning:

Project Title: Soundtrack Prediction with Machine Learning

This project focuses on utilizing machine learning to predict soundtracks based on diverse features like genre, mood, tempo, and instrumentation. Three models, Support Vector Machine (SVM), Random Forest, and Neural Network, are trained and compared for their ability to accurately predict soundtrack attributes. The project aims to enhance music recommendation systems and improve user satisfaction by providing personalized soundtrack suggestions.

4. Game Development:

Project Title: Doodle Jump Replica

This project involves creating a replica of the popular mobile game "Doodle Jump." Players guide a character upward by jumping on platforms while avoiding obstacles. Key features include platform generation, power-ups, scoring, graphics, sound effects, and cross-platform compatibility. The goal is to deliver an engaging gaming experience reminiscent of the original while incorporating personal touches and optimizations for various devices.
